My Top 5 Favorite Movies

  1. To All the Boys I've Loved Before
    • Year: 2018
    • Main Actors: Lana Condor, Noah Centineo, Anna Cathcart, Janel Parrish
    • Brief Description: To All the Boys I've Loved Before is a captivating story by Jenny Han that follows Lara Jean Covey as her secret love letters are unexpectedly mailed to her past crushes, turning her world upside down.
  2. The Maze Runner
    • Year: 2014
    • Main Actors: Dylan O'Brien, Kaya Scodelario, Will Poulter, Thomas Brodie-Sangster, Ki Hong Lee, Patricia Clarkson
    • Brief Description: The Maze Runner by James Dashner is a thrilling dystopian movie that follows the story of Thomas, who wakes up with no memory in a mysterious maze. As he navigates this dangerous labyrinth, he uncovers shocking secrets and fights for survival.
  3. Yeh Jawaani Hai Deewani
    • Year: 2013
    • Main Actors: Ranbir Kapoor, Deepika Padukone, Aditya Roy Kapoor, Kalki Koechlin, Kunaal Roy Kapur
    • Brief Description: "Yeh Jawaani Hai Deewani" is a Bollywood film about four friends who reunite for a trekking trip in the Himalayas. It explores themes of friendship, love, ambition, and self-discovery.
  4. Kingsman: The Secret Service
    • Year: 2014
    • Main Actors: Colin Firth, Taron Egerton, Samuel L. Jackson, Michael Caine, Mark Strong
    • Brief Description: A spy organisation recruits a promising street kid into the agency's training program, while a global threat emerges from a twisted tech genius.
  5. The Conjuring
    • Year: 2013
    • Main Actors: Patrick Wilson, Vera Farmiga, Sterling Jerins, Shannon Kook, Joey King, Mackenzie Foy, Shanley Caswell
    • Brief Description: Paranormal investigators Ed and Lorraine Warren work to help a family terrorized by a dark presence in their farmhouse.
